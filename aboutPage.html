Our Experience at EXPO 2020

About the Film

Let's go on a little Expo 2020 tour! Our Experience at EXPO 2020 is a film created during NYUAD's 30 Minutes Film Festival. The film shows clips of Salama and Keji's fun visit to Expo 2020's spectacular pavilions.

Morocco! The tall Moroccan pavilion designed by Tarik Oualalou replicates the houses of Moroccan villages stacked on top of each other and centered around a traditional Moroccan courtyard that supplies cool air throughout the building.
Saudi Arabia! The Saudi pavilion is the second largest pavilion at Expo 2020! The installations and displays inside are very impressive and the experience seems never ending. There is a lot to see and learn at this pavilion!
Singapore! A building ingulfed in all kinds of plants and trees inspired by Singapore's reputation as a "City in a Garden". The multi-layered green space is -- to experience and it reflects Singapore's aims on enviromental sustainability.
Belgium! The Belgian pavilion titled 'The Green Arch' for the arched, floral monolith that highlights the emergence of connecting green cities through industrial and scientific knowledge. The pavilian aims to displays ideas for the future of mobility, technology and innovation.
